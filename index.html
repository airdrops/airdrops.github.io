AirDrops
®

We put our interest and capacity into helping communities in remote locations. Social groups undergoing hunger and starvation. Our task, redesign the way food is packaged since during the process of dropping aid bags from airplanes many are lost due to impact. Plus the materials offer no re-usability for the communities that receive them. We test dropping bags with rice from a 50 meters crane. Using a high-speed camera for slow motion and sensors embedded inside the payload provide us with valid data we can analyze so to better understand the way the bags hit the ground.

Ideally the selected materials will become water filters, pipes or pieces of a kilt to build shelters.
