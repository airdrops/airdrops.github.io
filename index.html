Pier87/AirDrops
Many regions around the world are supplied with food by means of air-drops, a task done by diverse humanitarian organisations. The actual packing method represents lengthy work and high material costs, exceeding often the value of the food.

The Pier 87 team has put its interest and capacity into developing an alternative packing solution that optimises the use of resources and improves the techniques currently being used. Preliminary tests have shown the viability of the solution proposed and Pier 87 is currently moving toward field tests.
